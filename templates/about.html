About Yuzu Companion

A personal AI partner built with simplicity in mind

Yuzu Companion

Your affectionate AI partner system

Why I Built This

I wanted something simple, ready-to-go, and personal. No complicated setups, no subscription fees - just a companion that works out of the box.

Inspired by SillyTavern and other AI chat platforms, but stripped down to the essentials. The retro terminal vibe mixed with a clean web UI - because sometimes you want that nerd aesthetic, and sometimes you just want to chat.

Originally for personal use only... but who knows? Maybe someone else will find it useful too.

The Tech Stack
